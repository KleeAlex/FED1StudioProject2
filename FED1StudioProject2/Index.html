Who We Are

Here at Ghost Design we believe you can make anything for anyone. We are very passionate about heat and we want to share that with everyone. Here at Ghost Design we will teach you how to make all kinds of foods using Ghost Peppers, even desserts!

There's not much to say but we love what we do, and we hope you do too.
